When we set out to build Rangeway, we started with a simple belief: EV charging should be part of the journey, not an interruption to it. That philosophy shapes everything we do, from the climate-controlled Driver's Lounges at every location to the quality coffee waiting inside.

It should also shape how we look.

Today, we're introducing a refreshed Rangeway brand identity: a new logo, refined color palette, and typography system designed to reflect who we are and where we're headed.

## More Than a Letter

At the heart of our new identity is what we call the Path Mark. At first glance, it's a distinctive "R." Look closer, and you'll see a road extending forward, with a warm dot marking your destination.

That warmth isn't decorative. It represents the glow you see from the highway: the signal that comfort is just ahead. It's the lit window of a Driver's Lounge, the promise that this stop will be different from the cold, utilitarian charging experiences you've had before.

We explored several directions during the design process. Some leaned into mountain imagery and outdoor adventure aesthetics. They were attractive, but they weren't us. We're not an outdoor brand. We're a hospitality company that happens to serve travelers on America's highways.

The Path Mark captures that distinction. It says "destination" rather than "wilderness." It signals premium without pretension.

Together, they create a visual language that feels elevated yet inviting. Premium, but never cold.

The newest addition, Beacon, deserves special mention. This deep teal bridges our warm hospitality palette with the functional elements of our charging experience. You'll see it on wayfinding signage, hardware accents, and digital links. It's the color that says "this way to comfort."

## Typography With Purpose

We chose Raleway Bold for our wordmark and headlines. It's refined without being fussy, modern without chasing trends. The slight negative letter-spacing gives it a confident, grounded presence.

For body copy, Source Sans Pro provides the readability and warmth that hospitality communications require. Every word should feel considered, and the typography reinforces that intention.

## Why This Matters

A logo refresh might seem like a small thing. But for us, it represents clarity.

We think like hotel operators, not utility companies. We guarantee indoor comfort at every location, an industry first. We believe a 20-minute charging stop can be a moment worth savoring rather than time to endure.

Our new identity puts that philosophy front and center. When you see the Path Mark on a highway sign, you'll know exactly what's waiting: a Driver's Lounge with premium restrooms, comfortable seating, quality refreshments, and reliable charging. The warm dot is a promise.

## The Road Ahead

This refresh coincides with an exciting chapter for Rangeway. As we prepare to open our first locations and welcome our first Pathfinder Rewards members, we wanted our visual identity to match the experience we're building.

We're grateful to everyone who has supported us on this journey so far. Whether you're a future guest, a partner, or simply someone who believes EV travel should be better, this new brand is for you.

The road ahead looks bright. We hope you'll join us.
