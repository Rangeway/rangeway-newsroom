**SAN FRANCISCO, CA** — Rangeway, America's first hospitality-driven premium EV charging network, today announced its selection of Monta as its charge point management software (CPMS) partner. The partnership positions Rangeway among Monta's growing list of over twenty-five U.S. and Canadian network partners as the Copenhagen-founded platform completes its first full year in North America.

Monta's comprehensive platform will serve as the operational backbone for Rangeway's network of Basecamps and Waystations, managing everything from real-time charger monitoring and predictive diagnostics to seamless payment processing and dynamic pricing. The integration supports Rangeway's commitment to delivering consistent, premium experiences at every location.

"We evaluated charge point management platforms with the same standards we apply to every aspect of our guest experience," said Zak Winnick, Founder and CEO of Rangeway. "Monta stood out for its reliability, proven scale across advanced European markets, and the sophistication needed to support our Pathfinder Rewards program. Their platform gives us the foundation to deliver on our promise: that every Rangeway stop feels like part of the journey, not just a pause along the way."

"Rangeway is setting a new standard for EV charging in the hospitality sector, and we are proud that they have chosen Monta's platform as the engine for this ambitious network," explains Casper Rasmussen, co-founder and CEO of Monta. "Our software is built for reliability, ease of management, and scalability, which is precisely what is needed to ensure every customer's charging experience is seamless, efficient, and truly hospitality-driven."

Monta's OCPP 2.0.1-compliant platform brings capabilities essential to Rangeway's hospitality-first approach, including real-time monitoring and analytics across the entire network, dynamic pricing controls, bilateral roaming agreements, top rated driver app, and the integration infrastructure needed to power Pathfinder Rewards, Rangeway's earned-status loyalty program.

This partnership represents another step in Rangeway's mission to transform EV charging from a utility transaction into a hospitality experience. Unlike traditional charging networks that focus solely on infrastructure, Rangeway guarantees a climate-controlled Driver's Lounge with premium amenities at every location, setting a new standard for what EV travelers should expect on the road.

### About Rangeway

Rangeway is America's first hospitality-driven premium EV charging network, building destination experiences where EV charging becomes part of your journey. Every Rangeway location features a climate-controlled Driver's Lounge with premium restrooms, high-speed Wi-Fi, and comfortable seating, an industry first. The company operates two formats: Basecamps with staffed cafés and full amenities, and Waystations with automated retail and the same indoor comfort guarantee. Pathfinder Rewards, Rangeway's free-to-join loyalty program, lets members earn credits back and unlock experiential perks through their charging activity. Learn more at [rangewayev.com](https://rangewayev.com).

### About Monta

Monta established its North American headquarters in Miami in 2024 and has already become a key operating platform for more than twenty-five networks across North America, backed by strong Series B investment. Founded in Copenhagen in 2020, Monta is one of the world's leading EV charging software platforms, managing more than 250,000 commercial chargers and processing over three million monthly charging sessions. Learn more at [monta.com](https://monta.com).
