**IRVINE, CA** — Ekoenergetyka America today announced an exclusive partnership with Rangeway Energy, Inc., the pioneering EV charging company building America's first hospitality-driven premium charging network.

Under the agreement, Ekoenergetyka America will serve as the exclusive hardware provider for all Rangeway charging locations across the United States. The partnership brings Ekoenergetyka's proven high-power DC fast charging technology and European engineering expertise to Rangeway's growing network of Driver's Lounge destinations.

### Powering a New Charging Experience

Rangeway Energy is redefining what EV charging can feel like. Rather than competing on speed and price alone, Rangeway is building a hospitality-first network centered around climate-controlled indoor Driver's Lounges where guests can relax, work, or recharge themselves while their vehicles charge.

Delivering that elevated experience requires charging hardware built for long-term performance, reliability, and scalability.

"This partnership represents a major milestone for Rangeway", said Zak Winnick, Founder and CEO of Rangeway Energy. "We selected Ekoenergetyka America for their proven engineering, long-term reliability, and shared commitment to quality. Together, we are building a charging network that prioritizes both driver experience and operational excellence."

Ekoenergetyka America was selected following a comprehensive evaluation of global manufacturers. Key differentiators included

- Proven high-power DC fast charging technology
- Flexible product configurations supporting future growth
- Strong service support and long-term strategic alignment
- A shared commitment to premium user experience

"Our focus has always been on delivering charging infrastructure that performs reliably in real-world conditions," said Konrad konczewski, CEO of Ekoenergetyka America. "Rangeway's hospitality-driven model represents a bold step forward for the industry. We are proud to power a network that prioritizes both driver experience and operational excellence."

### Proven Technology, Expanding U.S. Presence

Ekoenergetyka has deployed thousands of high-power charging systems across Europe, supporting passenger vehicles, commercial fleets, and public transit operators. Through its North American headquarters in Irvine, California, the company is expanding its footprint to meet growing demand for reliable, scalable DC fast charging infrastructure.

Rangeway is currently developing sites in multiple states, with its first Ekoenergetyka-powered locations expected to come online in late 2026.

### Looking Ahead

This exclusive partnership marks a significant milestone for both companies and reinforces a shared vision: building charging infrastructure that is reliable, future-ready, and designed around the driver.

### About Ekoenergetyka America

Ekoenergetyka America is the North American subsidiary of Ekoenergetyka, supporting customers across the United States and Canada with local expertise and on the ground support. Based in the U.S., the company works closely with charging network operators, fleets, transit agencies, and infrastructure partners to deliver reliable DC fast charging solutions tailored to the North American market.

Ekoenergetyka is a leading European manufacturer of high power EV charging solutions with more than 15 years of experience in e mobility. Founded in 2009, the company serves customers in more than 40 countries across three continents and offers a portfolio of integrated all in one chargers and flexible split systems for EV, E Truck, and E Bus charging. Learn more at [ekoenergetyka.com/america](https://ekoenergetyka.com/america).

### About Rangeway

Rangeway is America's first hospitality-driven premium EV charging network, building destination experiences where EV charging becomes part of your journey. Every Rangeway location features a climate-controlled Driver's Lounge with premium restrooms, high-speed Wi-Fi, and comfortable seating, an industry first. The company operates two formats: Basecamps with staffed cafés and full amenities, and Waystations with automated retail and the same indoor comfort guarantee. Pathfinder Rewards, Rangeway's free-to-join loyalty program, lets members earn credits back and unlock experiential perks through their charging activity. Learn more at [rangewayev.com](https://rangewayev.com).
