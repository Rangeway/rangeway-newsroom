**SAN FRANCISCO, CA** -- Rangeway, America's first hospitality-driven premium EV charging network, today announced that **Red Canyon Roasting Company** will serve as the exclusive coffee program at every Rangeway Basecamp. Red Canyon, also founded by Rangeway CEO Zak Winnick, will roast and brew single-origin coffee on site inside each Basecamp's climate-controlled Driver's Lounge.

Red Canyon Roasting Company launched before Rangeway, born from Winnick's experience roasting coffee from the back of an electric truck powered by the vehicle itself. The roasting moved indoors, but the conviction stayed: source with care, roast in small batches, and never cut corners on quality. When Winnick later founded Rangeway, bringing Red Canyon into the Basecamp experience was a natural fit. The coffee program already existed, already met the standard, and already shared the same roots in the EV community.

The on-site roasting model sets the partnership apart from typical charging network food and beverage programs, which rely on pre-packaged products or third-party vendors. At a Rangeway Basecamp, drivers will watch beans being roasted and brewed in the same space where they charge, turning a routine stop into something worth remembering.

> "Red Canyon and Rangeway ask the same question: what does the best possible version of this experience look like?" said Zak Winnick, Founder and CEO of Rangeway. "We roast on site at every Basecamp. Not because it's easy, but because it's the standard we set for everything at Rangeway. Every detail of the experience should hold up to scrutiny, from the chargers to the restrooms to the coffee. If you're going to spend 20 minutes with us, the coffee in your hand should be worth that time."

## The Basecamp Coffee Experience

Rangeway Basecamps are the network's flagship format: full-service destinations featuring 2,500+ square feet of climate-controlled indoor space, staffed by hospitality-trained Trailkeepers, with comfortable seating, premium restrooms, and high-speed Wi-Fi. The addition of Red Canyon's on-site roasting operation brings a café experience built specifically for the 20- to 30-minute charging window, offering freshly roasted and brewed single-origin coffee alongside a curated food and beverage menu.

Red Canyon also operates independently as a direct-to-consumer coffee brand, roasting to order and shipping nationwide through **[shop.redcanyonroasting.co](https://shop.redcanyonroasting.co)**. The Rangeway Basecamp locations represent the only physical spaces where Red Canyon roasts on site.

## Sourcing and Sustainability

Red Canyon sources exclusively from farming cooperatives that practice sustainable cultivation. The company pays above Fair Trade minimum prices and reinvests a portion of every sale into the communities where its coffee is grown. Full details on Red Canyon's sourcing commitment are available at **[redcanyonroasting.co](https://redcanyonroasting.co)**.

## Pathfinder Rewards Integration

Red Canyon is fully integrated into Rangeway's Pathfinder Rewards loyalty program. Members at the Voyager tier and above receive a complimentary beverage at any Basecamp, making a freshly roasted Red Canyon coffee part of the earned experience for loyal drivers. Pathfinder Rewards is free to join, with status earned through usage across the entire Rangeway network.
